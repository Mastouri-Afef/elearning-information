ARES Scholarship in Belgium

About ARES Scholarship:

The ARES (Académie de Recherche et d'Enseignement Supérieur) scholarship program in Belgium offers opportunities for international students to pursue master's and training courses in various fields.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Applicants must be citizens of a developing country.
  • Must hold a Bachelor's degree relevant to the selected master's program.
  • Applicants should be below 40 years old for master's programs and 45 years for training programs.

Application Deadline:

The application deadline for the ARES scholarship is typically 17th November 2023 at 12:00 PM (Belgian time). Applicants are advised to check the official ARES website for the most up-to-date information.

How to Apply:

To apply for the ARES scholarship, candidates must follow these steps:

  1. Visit the official ARES website.
  2. Complete the online application form.
  3. Submit required documents, including academic transcripts and letters of recommendation.

Other Information:

  • The scholarship covers tuition fees, international travel expenses, living allowances, and insurance.
